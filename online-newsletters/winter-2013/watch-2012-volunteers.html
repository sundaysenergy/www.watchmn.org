in

WATCH is beyond lucky to have such a committed group of volunteers. When we look back on the year, all of our accomplishments were made possible because these committed individuals gave nearly 4,000 hours to monitor over 4,000 court hearings. They are an inspiring and integral part of improving the court’s response to violence against women and children, and we thank them all.
