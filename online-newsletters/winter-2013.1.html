If you want to learn more about the court system, are passionate about ending violence against women and children, or have simply decided that it‚Äôs time to get more involved, consider becoming a WATCH volunteer. Monitors and WATCH staff help the criminal justice system by identifying flaws, recommending solutions, and advocating for change.
