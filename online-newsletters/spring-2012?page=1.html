Local Courthouse Safety Act

in
  • Spring 2012

Minnesota senator Al Franken hosted a roundtable discussion at the Hennepin County Government Center on April 4 to discuss the Local Courthouse Safety Act, which he is sponsoring. The Act would allow local communities to use already allocated federal funds to enhance courthouse safety. It is specifically designed to improve security in smaller jurisdictions such as Cook County where a prosecutor was shot in December.
